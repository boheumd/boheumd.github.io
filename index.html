About Me

I am Bo He, a third-year Ph.D. student in the department of Computer Science at University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Prof. Abhinav Shrivastava. I received my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, China. During my undergraduate study, I was advised by Prof. Xilin Chen and Prof. Meina Kan in the VIPL Group at the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).

My research interests broadly includes deep learning and computer vision. I am especially interested in action recognition, video understanding and analysis.
